## Dot Clash mobile app

### Advertising consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

When required, Dot Clash shows Google's **User Messaging Platform (UMP)** consent form before ads load.

To change your choices later:

1. Open **Dot Clash**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Manage ad consent** (shown when required in your region)

### Device settings

You can also limit ad personalization in your device settings:

- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads

### Remove ads

Purchase **Remove Ads** in the in-app shop to disable interstitial ads.
