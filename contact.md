Need help with Dot Clash, your account, privacy, or ads?

## In the app (preferred)

1. Open **Dot Clash**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Contact us**
4. Fill in the form — we usually reply by email within a few business days

If you cannot use the app, email [{{ legal.contact_email }}](mailto:{{ legal.contact_email }}).

- A short description of your question
